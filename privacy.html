Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

At Spin Rewards Hub, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This document outlines the types of information collected and how we use it.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Spin Rewards Hub uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

Advertising Partners

Some of the advertisers on our site may use cookies and web beacons. Our advertising partners include Google AdSense and Newor Media. Each of our advertising partners has their own Privacy Policy for their policies on user data.
