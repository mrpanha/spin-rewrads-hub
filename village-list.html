🔥 What Are Boom Villages in Coin Master?

Boom Villages (also called Boom Levels) are specific villages in Coin Master where you have a higher-than-average chance of getting Rare and Gold Cards from Chests. Players who stay longer in these villages and open more chests tend to complete their card sets much faster.

Boom Villages were not officially confirmed by Moon Active for a long time, but community data collected from millions of chest openings has made the pattern very clear. There are two types:

💥 Regular Boom Villages

These villages provide a higher rate of rare cards. Opening chests here gives you a noticeably better chance of finding the cards you need.

⚡ Big Boom Villages

A step above regular Boom Villages — these give the highest possible card drop rates in the entire game. If you're hunting Gold Cards or completing a tough card set, stay in a Big Boom Village and open every chest you can.

💡 Pro Tip: When you reach a Boom Village, don't rush through it. Spend your coins on Chests and grind your card sets before moving on. The extra rare and gold card drops are worth more than the next village.

Based on community data, here are some of the well-confirmed Boom Villages: 15, 20, 30, 37, 50, 64, 75, 90, 100, 110, 120, 135, 143, 150, 160, 173, 175, 190, 200 and more. These are marked with 💥 in the table above. Big Boom Villages (⚡) include confirmed high-value levels such as 50, 100, 143, 173.

💰 How to Save Coins on Village Builds

Village costs in Coin Master grow exponentially. By Level 200, you're spending over 100 billion coins per village. Here's how to build smarter:

1. Build During Village Mania

Village Mania is the single best event for building. There are two types: a 20% discount on all items, or a massive 65% discount on the bottom-row items (which are always the most expensive). Never skip Village Mania.

2. Stack Village Master on Top

Village Master rewards you with free Spins, Coins, and XP when you complete your village upgrade list. Time your completion to happen during Village Master for bonus rewards on top of the Mania discount.

3. Build All 5 Items in One Session

This is critical for defense. If you build one item at a time, other players can raid you overnight and force you to spend coins on repairs. Save enough coins to complete all 5 village items in a single play session. Once all items are upgraded, your coins are protected inside the buildings.

4. Use Foxy During Raids

Before building, use Foxy (your pet) to maximise your Raid earnings. Foxy gives you extra coin digs during Raids, helping you stack coins faster for the next village.

⚡ Event Stack (2026 Meta): Build in bulk during Village Mania for discounts, then complete the village during Village Master for double rewards. If Build Rush overlaps, earn extra milestones and boosters simultaneously.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many villages are there in Coin Master in 2026?
As of March 2026, there are over 570 villages in Coin Master. Moon Active (the developer) regularly adds new villages through game updates, so this number continues to grow.
What is the most expensive village in Coin Master?
The most expensive villages are the highest-level ones (Village 500+). Costs at those levels can exceed hundreds of trillions of coins. The costs grow exponentially as you progress through the game.
Do village costs change based on my Star count?
There are player reports suggesting that village costs scale based on your total Star count — the more Stars you have, the more expensive upgrades become. This has not been officially confirmed by Moon Active, but you can estimate your own costs by checking the last item in your Village Shop menu.
What happens after you complete all villages?
Once you've built every available village, all that's left is to stockpile coins and wait for Moon Active to add new villages in future updates. There's no special end-game reward — the game is designed to always add more content.
Can I reduce the cost of building a village?
Yes! The best way is to build during Village Mania events, which offer 20% or 65% discounts. The Golden Pass also helps offset costs. There is no permanent way to reduce village prices — only event-based discounts work.
What are Gold Cards and why are they important?
Gold Cards are the rarest cards in Coin Master. Unlike regular cards, Gold Cards cannot be traded with other players normally — you need a special Gold Card Trade event. Completing a card set that includes Gold Cards unlocks thousands of free Spins and major rewards. Boom Villages are the best place to hunt Gold Cards from Chests.
